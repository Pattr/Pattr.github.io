Pattr is all about HTML

The only sensible way to assure the success of a web site is by starting with a standard and semantic markup. Pattr components will help you getting started with the right foot.

A Library with Accessibility in its core

Pattr components will give you the hints you need to allow accessibility drive your project from the very start.

Markup spiced up with microformats 2

We love microformats and it paves the cowpaths to enhance access to data. Its power comes from its simplicity and its respect to web standards. That's why we spiced up our markup with its latest version, there where it was pertinent.

Spreading the good code

We're relentless in following the evolution of web technologies, but yet, you're not going to find in Pattr outstanding animations or that super original experience with canvas. Instead, you'll get a standard, structured and accessible markup, carefully crafted to allow you to enhance your experience on top of it.

This is where your progressive enhanced project begins.
